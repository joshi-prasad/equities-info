## 1. COMPANY PROFILE

TVS Motor Company Limited is India's third-largest two-wheeler manufacturer. Products: Apache, Ronin, Raider (sports/premium), Jupiter, Ntorq (scooters), Star City+ (commuter), iQube/TVS X/TVS Orbiter (EV), King 3-wheelers, BMW G310 (manufacturing partner), Norton Motorcycles (UK, owned). FY26 volumes: 58.89 lakh units (+24% YoY) — highest ever. Exports FY26: 15.85 lakh units (+33% YoY) — highest ever. Q3 FY26: Revenue INR 12,476 Cr (+37% YoY), EBITDA INR 1,634 Cr (13.1% margin — highest-ever), PAT INR 940 Cr (+52% YoY).

## 2. CORE GROWTH DRIVERS

### Driver 1: Exports — 33% Volume Growth, 35% Revenue Growth, Best-Ever FY26

FY26 export volumes: 15.85 lakh units (+33% YoY). Key markets: Africa (recovery), Sri Lanka (revival), Latin America (white space gains). Q3 FY26 exports: +35% YoY vs industry +23%. Currency tailwind throughout FY26. Mix improvement (Apache, Ntorq, iQube = higher ASP).

**Evidence**
- Axis Direct Q3: "Exports +35% YoY — outperforming industry +23%. Africa recovery, Sri Lanka revival, Latin America traction."
- FY26 volume press release: "Total exports 15.85 lakh units (+33% YoY)."

---

### Driver 2: EV — iQube #2 Position, +54% Volume Growth in Q4

iQube: #2 EV scooter in India. 1.1 lakh units/quarter Q3 FY26 (+40% YoY). TVS Orbiter: entry-level EV, expanding TAM. TVS X: premium EV. Q4 FY26 EV volumes: 0.76 lakh units (+54% YoY). FY26 EV sales: 2.8 lakh+ units (from 1.93 lakh FY25).

**Evidence**
- Axis Direct Q3: "EV 2W sales 1.1 lakh units (+40% YoY). Orbiter ramping rapidly."
- Bajaj Broking Q4: "EV volumes 0.76 lakh units (+54% YoY). EBITDA margin 14.0% (+270 bps)."

---

### Driver 3: EBITDA Margin Expansion — 13.1% (Highest-Ever)

FY19 margin: ~8%. H1 FY26: 12.6%. Q3 FY26: 13.1% (highest ever). Q4 FY26 consolidated: 14.0% (+270 bps YoY). Driven by PLI (0.5% of revenue), Apache/iQube/Ntorq premium mix, and operating leverage. ICICI estimates 19%/24% Revenue/EBITDA CAGR over FY25–FY28E.

---

### Driver 4: Norton + BMW Partnership — Premium Portfolio

Norton Motorcycles (UK) revived under TVS. BMW G310 manufactured at Hosur, exported to 50+ markets. Both provide brand halo + export revenue diversification.

## 4. KEY RISKS

1. Commodity inflation: Input cost inflation ~20% YoY. Margin risk in FY27.
2. EV competition: Ola Electric, Bajaj Chetak, Ather competing on iQube's turf.
3. Middle East logistics: Freight costs rising, longer transit times.
4. Norton monetisation timeline.
5. GST base effect: H1 FY26 domestic growth partly GST-tailwind driven.
