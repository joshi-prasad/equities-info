## 2. CORE GROWTH DRIVERS

### Driver 1: 500 MMT Milestone — First Indian Operator

- APSEZ became **first Indian operator to handle 500 MMT in a single year**.
- Q4 FY26 cargo: **133.5 MMT** (+13% YoY).
- April FY27 (first month): **43.1 MMT** (+**15% YoY**) — containers +17%, dry cargo +17%.
- All-India container market share: **45.5%** in FY26.
- Domestic port capacity: **653 MMT** as of March 2026.

### Driver 2: Logistics — Asset-Light, 62–81% Revenue Growth

- Q3 FY26 logistics revenue: ₹1,121 Cr (+**62% YoY**).
- 9M FY26 logistics revenue: +**81% YoY**.
- Q4 FY26 logistics EBITDA margin: improved **250 bps** to **20.1%**.
- Transformation: port operator → **Integrated Transport Utility** ("shore-to-door").
- FY26 trucking guided to grow **3x–4x YoY**.

### Driver 3: Marine — +91% Q3 Revenue

- Q3 FY26 marine revenue: **₹773 Cr** (+91% YoY); EBITDA ₹428 Cr (+135% YoY).
- Ongoing vessel acquisitions; fleet supports port volumes and third-party service.
- Captive marine capability reduces third-party dependence, adds high-margin revenue.

### Driver 4: International Ports — All-Time High ₹4,539 Cr, +34% FY26

- Q3 FY26: First quarter >₹1,000 Cr (₹1,067 Cr, +20% YoY); EBITDA doubled.
- Q4 FY26: ₹1,422 Cr (+58% YoY — all-time high quarterly).
- FY26 full year: **₹4,539 Cr** (+34% YoY) — now ~12% of group revenue.
- Colombo West International Terminal (CWIT): South/Southeast Asia transshipment hub.

### Driver 5: FY29 Vision — Revenue ₹65,500 Cr (Double FY26), 1,000 MMT

- FY29 targets: Revenue **₹65,500 Cr**, EBITDA **₹36,500 Cr** (both ~double FY26).
- Volume target: **1,000 MMT** (2x FY26).
- Levers: capacity expansion, logistics scaling, marine fleet, Colombo/Vizhinjam ramp, new international ports.

## 4. KEY RISKS

1. **Geopolitical (Haifa/Israel):** Conflict risk; Haifa operations exposed.
2. **Container volume headwinds:** Morbi weakness; high freight costs delaying exporters.
3. **Margin dilution:** Logistics/marine mix shift compresses blended margins short-term.
4. **Leverage:** High capex; Net Debt/EBITDA 1.9x must be monitored.
5. **Regulatory/concession risk:** Port concessions granted by government; renewal risk.
6. **Adani Group overhang:** Group-level events affect financing and sentiment.
