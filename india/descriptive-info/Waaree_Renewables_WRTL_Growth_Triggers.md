**Sources: Q4 FY26 financial results (Kalkine, April 16, 2026); FY26 full-year results (PHD Capital LinkedIn); Q3 FY26 earnings call (AlphaSpread/NSE, January 16, 2026); GrowthVista Q4 analysis; VariantInvestor podcast.**

---

## 1. COMPANY PROFILE

Waaree Renewable Technologies Limited (WRTL) is the EPC and services subsidiary of the Waaree Group — distinct from Waaree Energies. WRTL executes solar EPC, O&M, rooftop, C&I, floating solar, IPP, and BESS EPC. FY26: Revenue INR 3,331 Cr (+108% YoY), PAT INR 479 Cr (+109% YoY), EBITDA INR 641 Cr (+110% YoY, 19.24% margin). Q4 FY26: Revenue INR 1,102 Cr (+131% YoY), PAT INR 156 Cr (+66% YoY), EBITDA margin 18.76%.

---

## 2. CORE GROWTH DRIVERS

### Driver 1: Utility-Scale Solar EPC — 2.23 GW Executed in 9M FY26

2.23 GW executed in 9M FY26 — extraordinary pace. Unexecuted order book: 2.92 GW (December 2025). Q4 FY26 wins: 420 MWp + 35 MWp + 14 MWp ground-mounted solar. EPC segment profit up 2.2x YoY in FY26. India's 44.6 GW solar addition in FY26 creates structural pipeline.

**Evidence**
- Q3 FY26 call: "Unexecuted order book: 2.92 GW. Executed 2,230 MWp in 9M FY26."
- Q4 FY26 (Kalkine): "Revenue INR 1,102.40 Cr (+131.31% YoY). EPC primary driver."
- PHD Capital: "Revenue doubled. EPC segment profit up 2.2x."

---

### Driver 2: O&M Portfolio — 1,180 MWp Recurring Revenue

O&M portfolio: 1,180 MWp (December 2025). Fees: ~INR 2–4 lakh/MW/year — high-margin recurring stream. Every 1 GW commissioned adds ~INR 20–40 Cr/year permanent O&M backlog.

---

### Driver 3: IPP Portfolio — 227 MWp Under Development

227.10 MWp IPP portfolio under development. IPP revenue: 2–3% of total; growing over FY27–FY29. C&I offtake at INR 3.5–5/unit under long-term PPA.

---

### Driver 4: BESS EPC — Government-Mandated Segment

Government mandating 2-hour BESS with new solar tenders. WRTL executing BESS EPC using Waaree Energies group BESS manufacturing. Same customer relationships → higher contract value per project.

**Evidence**
- Q3 FY26 call: "Company now executing BESS EPC work. Rising demand for integrated solar and storage projects."

## 4. KEY RISKS

1. **Working capital (PHD Capital)**: Receivables grew 2.4x vs revenue 2x. Operating cash flow flat at INR 287 Cr despite PAT doubling.
2. **EPC margin compression**: Competition from L&T, Tata Power Solar, in-house EPC teams.
3. **Revenue lumpiness**: EPC project milestone payments create uneven quarters.
4. **Parent dependency**: Brand, balance sheet, and module supply from Waaree Energies.
