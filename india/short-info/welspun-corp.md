# Welspun Corp Ltd. – Investment Summary

## Company Snapshot

Welspun Corp is a global infrastructure-enabling company specializing in:

* Large-diameter line pipes
* Ductile Iron (DI) pipes
* Stainless steel tubes and bars
* Building materials through Sintex

The company should not be viewed as a commodity steel manufacturer.

Instead, Welspun operates as a:

**Qualification-driven infrastructure supplier benefiting from global energy, water, and infrastructure investment cycles.**

Its competitive advantage comes from:

* Global certifications
* Local manufacturing presence in USA and Saudi Arabia
* Complex project execution capabilities
* Long-standing customer relationships
* Supply-chain reliability

The business currently derives most of its earnings from:

* U.S. line pipe operations
* Saudi associate operations (EPIC)

while simultaneously investing in:

* Saudi greenfield facilities
* U.S. capacity expansion
* Sintex building materials

to create future growth engines.

---

# Core Investment Thesis

The investment thesis is NOT steel prices.

Welspun operates largely on a:

**Cost-pass-through conversion model.**

Raw material costs are hedged immediately after order booking, making profitability largely independent of steel price volatility.

The current opportunity is driven by:

* U.S. gas infrastructure expansion
* AI data-center related power demand
* LNG infrastructure investments
* Saudi Vision 2030
* Saudi import-substitution opportunities
* U.S. capacity shortages
* Operating leverage
* Negative working-capital dynamics through customer advances

Over the last several years Welspun invested heavily in:

* U.S. manufacturing footprint
* Saudi manufacturing presence
* Product certifications
* Coating capabilities
* Sintex platform expansion

The business has now transitioned from:

**Capacity Creation Phase → Monetization Phase**

The key question is no longer:

**"Can Welspun win orders?"**

The key question is:

**"Can Welspun sustain peak utilization, execute new capacity expansions and convert backlog into cash flow while maintaining returns?"**

The dominant profit contributor.

Products include:

* HSAW pipes

* Future LSAW pipes

Key demand drivers:

* AI data-center power infrastructure
* Natural gas pipelines
* LNG infrastructure
* Midstream investments

Key characteristics:

* Highest margins
* Highest ROCE
* Strongest backlog visibility
* Significant customer advances

The U.S. mill is effectively booked through FY28.

## 2. Saudi Arabia Operations (Second Profit Engine)

Consists of:

* EPIC JV operations
* New LSAW facility
* New DI pipe facility

Key demand drivers:

* Saudi Aramco investments
* Water infrastructure
* Vision 2030 projects

## 3. India Line Pipes

Manufactures:

* LSAW pipes

* Water transmission projects

---

## 5. Sintex (Future Growth Platform)

Products include:

* Water tanks
* UPVC pipes

* OPVC pipes

This segment is strategically important because it transitions Welspun from:

**Project-based B2B revenue**

toward

**Recurring B2C demand.**

The long-term objective is to expand the addressable market from roughly:

₹12,000 Cr → ₹85,000 Cr

through broader building-material participation.

# Why The Moat Exists

The moat is not steel manufacturing.

The moat is:

**Qualification + Certifications + Geographic Presence + Execution Reliability**

Key evidence:

* API approvals
* Saudi Aramco approvals
* Norsok certifications
* Aerospace certifications
* Local U.S. manufacturing
* Local Saudi manufacturing
* Long customer relationships
* Complex coating capabilities

The most important moat is:

**Being a globally approved supplier located inside protected markets such as the U.S. and Saudi Arabia.**

Factories can be replicated.

Customer trust, certifications, and localization advantages cannot.

# Financial Inflection

FY26 appears to be the major financial inflection year.

### Key developments

* EBITDA reached record levels
* EBITDA margins expanded to ~14%
* ROCE exceeded 22%
* Operating cash flow surged to ₹3,204 Cr
* Net debt turned into net cash
* Working capital became negative
* Customer advances increased significantly
* U.S. order visibility extended through FY28

The most important development:

**Customer advances transformed working-capital economics and significantly improved capital efficiency.**

# Bull / Base / Bear Framework

## Bull Case

* U.S. backlog extends beyond FY28
* AI-related gas infrastructure remains strong
* Saudi facilities ramp successfully
* Saudi anti-dumping measures accelerate adoption
* Sintex scales faster than expected
* ROCE remains above 20%

## Base Case

* U.S. demand remains healthy
* Saudi facilities ramp gradually
* India water demand recovers
* Sintex expands steadily
* Working capital remains efficient
* ROCE stays around current levels

## Bear Case

* U.S. gas infrastructure cycle slows
* AI power demand shifts away from gas
* Customer advances decline
* Saudi ramp-up disappoints
* Sintex expansion underperforms
* ROCE compresses toward historical levels

# Final Investment View

Welspun Corp appears to be transitioning from:

* a cyclical pipe manufacturer

toward

* a diversified infrastructure platform with exposure to:

  * Energy infrastructure
  * Water infrastructure
  * Saudi localization
  * Building materials

The current earnings engine remains the U.S. line pipe business, benefiting from:

* AI-related power infrastructure
* LNG investments
* Supply shortages
* Local manufacturing advantages

The next phase of value creation will likely come from:

* U.S. capacity expansion
* Saudi localization
* Saudi import substitution
* Sintex scale-up

The key question is no longer:

**"Can Welspun generate demand?"**

The key question is:

**"Can Welspun successfully convert exceptional demand visibility into sustained earnings, cash flow and capital-efficient growth?"**

If management succeeds, FY27-FY29 could see continued improvement in:

* Earnings
* Cash generation
* ROCE
* International market share
* Business diversification

The single most important variable to monitor is:

**U.S. Order Book Duration and its conversion into Revenue, Cash Flow and ROCE.**
